The Commutative Property says that you can add or multiply numbers in any order and the result is the same: 3 x 4 = 4 x 3.

The Zero Property of Multiplication says that whenever you multiply by 0, the result is 0: 4 x 0 = 0.

The Identity Property of Multiplication says that whenever you multiply a number by 1, the result is that number: 12 x 1 = 12.
